# sApIens

sApIens is a process-first AI tutoring project focused on helping students develop real understanding rather than simply producing correct answers.

Instead of giving solutions, sApIens guides students through their reasoning, asks targeted questions, and adapts support based on how a student is thinking.

## Core Principles
- Process over outcomes
- Step-by-step reasoning
- Misconception-aware guidance
- Active, reflective learning

## Goal
To explore how AI can act as a tutor that helps students learn how to think, not just what to answer.
