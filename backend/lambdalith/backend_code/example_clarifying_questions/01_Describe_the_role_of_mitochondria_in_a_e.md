# Clarifying Questions — Example 1

**Grade / Subject:** 7th grade Life Science

**Student Question:**
> Describe the role of mitochondria in a eukaryotic cell.

## Generated Clarifying Questions

- What is a eukaryotic cell?
- What does "role" mean?
- How long should I write?
- Should I include a diagram?
- How much detail is required?
- Are mitochondria in all eukaryotes?
