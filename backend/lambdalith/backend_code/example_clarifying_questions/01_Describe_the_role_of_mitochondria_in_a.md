# Clarifying Questions — Example 1

**Grade / Subject:** 7th grade Life Science

**Student Question:**
> Describe the role of mitochondria in a eukaryotic cell.

## Generated Clarifying Questions

- What does "role" mean in this context — am I describing what mitochondria do, what they look like, or both?
- What does "eukaryotic" mean?
- Should my answer focus on one main function or cover everything mitochondria do?
- Is it okay to use the word "energy" in my answer, or do I need to be more specific?
- Are mitochondria found in all eukaryotic cells, or only some?
