# Clarifying Questions — Example 5

**Grade / Subject:** 7th grade Life Science

**Student Question:**
> What happens to chromosomes during mitosis, and why is this important for the organism?

## Generated Clarifying Questions

- What does mitosis mean?
- What are chromosomes?
- How detailed should I be?
- List steps or write paragraph?
- Specific organisms or general?
- How many reasons for importance?
