# Clarifying Questions — Example 2

**Grade / Subject:** 7th grade Life Science

**Student Question:**
> What is the difference between active transport and passive transport across a cell membrane?

## Generated Clarifying Questions

- What does "transport" mean in the context of a cell?
- Does my answer need to include specific examples of each type, or just describe them generally?
- What does "across a cell membrane" mean — is it asking about things moving in, out, or both directions?
- How detailed does my answer need to be?
- Is "concentration gradient" a term I should know for this question?
- Does "difference" mean I should compare them side by side, or describe each one separately?
