# Clarifying Questions — Example 4

**Grade / Subject:** 7th grade Life Science

**Student Question:**
> How do producers, consumers, and decomposers interact in a food web?

## Generated Clarifying Questions

- What is a "food web" — is it the same as a food chain, or different?
- Do I need to give specific examples of producers, consumers, and decomposers, or just describe them in general?
- Does "interact" mean I should explain what each one does to the others, or just what each one does on its own?
- Should my answer describe all three groups or is it okay to focus on one pair at a time?
